How it works

The end-to-end processes of the national city finance rankings 2022, involving data
collection, review and approval, as well as computation of scores and ranks.

All ULBs are required to submit their key financial data and upload their financial documents on the www.cityfinance.in platform.

Facilitate peer learnings by building a collaborative and learning environment that would enable scaling best oractises across ULBs.
