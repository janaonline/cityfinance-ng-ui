Introduction

Guidelines to Urban Local Bodies (ULBs):

  1. ULBs need enter data and upload documents appropriately. Targets for service level indicators and required documents must be uploaded after taking due approval from Municipal commissioner/ Executive officer of ULB.
  2. Only one PDF document can be uploaded against each requirement. If more than one document needs to be uploaded for a requirement, you need to merge the file and then upload.
  3. Data validation are built in the cell where you need to enter data. Please enter data in required format
  4. Refer the resources when you have any doubt.
  5. ULBs can enter data and upload documents at their convenience i.e. at one go or in several sessions by saving it as draft.
  6. It would be easier for ULBs to put all the documents that are required to be entered and uploaded in a single folder before start entering or uploading.
  7. You need to name the documents to be uploaded in the following format “Document Name_State Name_ULB Name.
  8. ULBs may see the preview of the data entered and documents uploaded before submitting it.
  9. ULBs would be able to take a print out of the data entered for their future reference.
  10. ULBs would not be able to edit the form after submitting it.

Guidelines for Nodal officer from State:

  1. The Nodal officer from the state for the 15th FC Grant for ULBs is required to evaluate/ review the baseline & targets for service level benchmarks and required documents for 15th FC Grant. The Nodal officer will Approve/ Reject the claims based on the evaluation.
  2. If submission by ULB is in order, the Approve button shall be ticked. Each submission/ document by ULB has to be approved/ rejected.
  3. After approval the submissions by ULBs will be forwarded to the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) for final approval.
  4. If submission by ULB is not in order, then the Reject button shall be ticked and the accompanying reason for rejection must be entered.
  5. If one or more submissions made by the ULB are rejected, the form will be sent back to the ULB for correction and re-submission.
  6. Nodal officer will not be able to enter any data or submit any documents on behalf of the ULBs.
  7. Refer the user manual in case of any doubt.
  8. Nodal officer may see the preview of the Approve/ Reject decisions recorded before final submission.
  9. Nodal officer will be able to take a print out of the decisions recorded for future reference from the preview page.
  10. States/ nodal officer will not be able to edit the form after submitting it.

Guidelines to MoHUA:

  1. MoHUA is required to evaluate/ review the submissions by the ULBs after approval of the State as per guidelines for 15th FC grant for ULBs.
  2. If MoHUA is satisfied with the submissions made by the ULB, the Approve button shall be ticked.
  3. If MoHUA is not satisfied with the submissions made by the ULB, the Reject button shall be ticked and the accompanying reason for rejection must be entered.
  4. If one or more claims made by the ULB are rejected, the form will be sent back to the ULB for correction and re-submission. The resubmitted claims will need to be first approved by the State before it arrives for MoHUA’s decision.
  5. MoHUA will not be able to enter any data or submit any documents on behalf of the ULBs.
  6. MoHUA may see the preview of the Approve/ Reject decisions recorded before submitting it.
  7. MoHUA will be able to take a print out of the decisions recorded for their future reference.
  8. MoHUA will not be able to edit the form after submitting it.
