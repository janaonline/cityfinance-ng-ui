Information Required on property tax and user charges for Ministry of Finance scheme

With reference to the scheme launched by Ministry of Finance for additional borrowing, States are required to undertake the following reforms as prerequisites for additional borrowing limit of 0.25 percent of GSDP for the year 2020-21.

(1) Notify:
(a) floor rates of property tax in ULBs which are in consonance with the prevailing circle rates (i.e. guideline rates for property transactions) and
(b) floor rates of user charges in respect of the provision of water-supply, drainage and sewerage which reflect current costs/past inflation

(2) Put in place a system of periodic increase in floor rates of property tax/user charges in line with the price increases

The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is the nodal ministry to assess the progress of these reforms and recommend release of additional borrowing. We require the following information from states as the first step towards implementing the scheme.
