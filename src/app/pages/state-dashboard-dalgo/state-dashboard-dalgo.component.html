Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides a graphical representation of revenue and expenditure line items from FY 2019–20 to FY 2022–23. The data is based on submissions from various cities, with extrapolated values where direct data was unavailable.

National-level

Summary of demographics and municipal (urban) indicators.

State-level

Summary of demographics and municipal (urban) indicators at the state level.

ULB-type-level

(Urban Local Body) summary of demographics and municipal (urban) indicators.

The dashboard enables users to:

  • Compare the progress of one city against another
  • Benchmark states against other states
  • Understand trends and performance over the selected years

By default, the dashboard displays data for the year 2019–20.

Notes

Data spans FY 2019–20 through FY 2022–23 and uses extrapolation where city-level inputs were not available. Use filters in the dashboard to change year, geography (national/state/ULB-type), and compare cities or states side-by-side.
