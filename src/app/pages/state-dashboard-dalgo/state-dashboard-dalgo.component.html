Welcome to the State Dashboard

This dashboard presents revenue and expenditure data from FY 2019–20 to FY 2022–23, enabling comparative and trend-based insights across cities and states.

Data Source: Audited financial statements submitted by ULGs as part of 15th Finance Commission compliance. To ensure comparability across states/cities, wherever direct ULG level data was unavailable, values have been extrapolated using per capita estimates across population categories.

The default year has been fixed to FY 2019–20 for baseline comparison. With this dashboard, you can:

  • Compare the progress of one city against another
  • Benchmark states against other states
  • Understand trends and performance for chosen years
