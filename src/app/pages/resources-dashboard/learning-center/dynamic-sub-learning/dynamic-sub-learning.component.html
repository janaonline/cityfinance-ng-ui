What is the Property Tax Digital Toolkit?
This toolkit is a one-stop-shop for all your needs for reforming property taxes in your State/ULB. It provides reform recommendations based on best-practices implemented nationally and internationally and provides a step-wise implementation guide that you can easily follow to take immediate action. Reform recommendations in each stage of the lifecycle of property tax have been framed as responses to issues and implementation challenges, sharply addressing WHAT NEEDS to BE DONE and HOW CAN IT BE DONE.

Current Practices

Collection efficiency is dependent on the completeness of billing and administrative efficiencies in the collection process. In many ULBs, there is currently no process in place to ensure completeness of billing, timely billing and issuance of payment reminders for payment. Several ULBs continue to maintain manual records that are vulnerable to errors. Significant staff vacancies in revenue departments also adversely impact collection efficiencies. Some cities that have created an online billing and collection system through procurement of services of an external vendor have difficulties in managing the system due to inadequate capacity building measures being undertaken for revenue officials. Adoption of online payment of property tax has also been relatively slow and varies across cities.
