About

Cityfinance.in is an initiative of Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India. The primary objective of CityFinance.in is to serve as a national framework of standardised, timely and credible financial information on cities, beginning with urban local bodies. The information to be covered would in a phased manner include balance sheet and income & expenditure information from annual accounts, budgeted receipts and payments vs actuals from annual budgets, progress of tenders and civic works, service level benchmarks, CAG and DLFA audit reports, credit rating reports, details of municipal bond issuances, model RFPs, compilation of best practices in revenue enhancement and digital learning modules. We hope the portal is useful as a crucial support system for Principal Secretaries, Directors of Municipal Administration, Municipal Commissioners, Smart City CEOs, Investors, Merchant Bankers, Project Consultants, Credit Rating Agencies & Banks.

Benefits for States/Cities

Enhance own revenues

Gain access to best practices and innovations, besides benchmarking the own revenue performance your organisation with other urban local bodies and civic agencies in your peer group.

Raise market financing

Look up comparative financial and performance information of urban local bodies and civic agencies, credit rating reports, details of municipal bond issuances and database of Public Private Partnerships in urban infrastructure and services.

Improve financial management and budgetary control

Refer model RFPs, technical documents and model policies for accounting, budgeting, MIS and auditing for best in class financial management and budgetary control

Build skills and competencies

Get started on reform initiatives and innovations with ready to use playbooks, sign-up for digital learning modules
