About

Cityfinance.in is an initiative of Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India. The primary objective of this portal is to create a national database of city finances and design framework for analysing the financial and operational performance of the cities. CityFinance.in serves as a nationally standardized and comparable framework of city finances, that is credible and timely. It portrays financial and operational performance information such as income, expenditure, assets, liabilities, budgets vs actuals, progress of tenders and civic works and Service Level Benchmarks. The stakeholders of the portal include Principal Secretaries - Urban Development, Directors of Municipal Administration, Municipal Commissioners, Smart City CEOs, Investors, Merchant Bankers, Project Consultants, Credit Rating Agencies & Banks.

Revenue enhancement

Provides access to best practices regarding improved collection efficiencies, alternative/innovative revenue sources

Improved financial management & budgetary control

Makes available model RFPs for activities like empanelment of CAs for audit, adoption of ERP accounting systems, etc.

Infrastructure financing

Serves as a data repository for investors/merchant bankers to help them evaluate the ecosystem Facilitates a boost in flow of funds through Municipal Bonds, Pool Debt Obligation, PPPs etc.

Capacity building

Assists in bridging skill gaps through playbooks & FAQs on PPPs, revenue enhancement, municipal bonds, etc. Assists in capacity building of municipal accounting staff through E-learning modules and certification
